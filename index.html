Projects

Hack Cube

HackCube is a service for hackathon hosters to have better control of their Hackathon. Attendees and Hosters have a variety of features. For example, an attendee can request help from mentors by looking at a list of mentors who are available. The hosters have the ability to notify everyone about important updates such as when the food arrives, etc.

Class Vibes

A service to allow teachers to ask their students how they are feeling. When the students answer, the teacher gets a real-time graph update with three colors. Yello for Ok, red for needs help, and green for good. The teacher also has the ability to schedule a meeting and send announcements to the class.

Dev Space

A social platform for developers to collaborate. DevSpace allows developers to share their projects, post-tech-related discussions, and connect with other developers to collaborate on projects. The app includes a home screen where developers can view posts and projects of other developers as well as contact them. It is a platform developers to meet with each other and collaborate on future projects. There is a direct messaging feature that allows them to contact one another included in the app.

G- Alert

An app that allows students in a school to alarms all the other students if there is a suspicious threat in the school. For example an active shooter, an unknown person on campus, etc. They have the ability to view all exit routes and quick contacts to their parents and 911.

Delivery Deck

Delivery deck is an app that allows any company to hire regular people to deliver their products for them. Its like an uber for delivering products. This would greatly reduce the price instead of using large postal services such as UPS or Fedex.

Par Car

An app that has an interesting methon for reserving parking spaces. The app works by reserving a parking space. The driver than parks in that spot and waits there until the other member arrives. When he/she arrive, they switch spots. Thus gauranteein your parking spot every time.

Civita

An app that allows event hosters to post an event on the app requesting unpaid volunteering. This allows a place for students to find opportunities for community service hours to meet their graduating requirements.
